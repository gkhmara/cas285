Daily walks, runs, and a lot of fun!

Hello, My name is Amanda and I am the founder of Wild at Heart Dog Walking & Pet-sitting. Wild at Heart is dedicated to keeping Portland's pets healthy and happy. We are comfortable handling all kinds of pets. Wild at Heart is fully insured and has over fifteen years professional experience. We strive to promote an ethic of compassion in every aspect of our organization. We believe in livable wages, therefore we are a cooperative. All of our staff are equal owners. We support local businesses and local animal welfare organizations. Check out our "About" page to learn more about us!
